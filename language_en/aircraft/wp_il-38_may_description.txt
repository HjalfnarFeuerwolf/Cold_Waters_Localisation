History=Most aptly described as the russian contemporary of the US Lockheed P-3, the Il-38 is a medium-range antisubmarine aircraft, derived from the Il-18 turboprop airliner. They are capable of laying huge barriers of sonoboys, and then pressing home the attack with an assortment of depth charges and homing torpedoes.
